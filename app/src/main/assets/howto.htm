How to use?


Add icon inside your quick actions toolbar.
After selecting it in any app, your touchscreen is now locked.

To unlock, click 3 times.

To play/pause simply click and swipe up

To toggle screen off mode when covering proximity sensor, click and swipe down. This mode is useful for saving battery when listening to music from YouTube app while having phone in your pocket or with screen facing down on table.
